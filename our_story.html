Our story

Since the beginning, it’s been our
focus to help people build better.

⬤ OUR MISSION
[1]Find stronger, more sustainable alternatives to natural stone, [2] evolve and enhance the way we work with composite materials, and [3]spark endless creativity.

It began with a belief—that even the most overlooked parts of a city could be reimagined with intention and beauty. In 1994, this belief led to the creation of the world’s first composite drain cover. And it’s what continues to guide us today.

⬤ PRESENT DAY

A generation on, we’re still challenging what’s possible in urban design—shaped by our roots in Singapore, a city at the crossroads of East and West.

“At Jonite, we believe innovation thrives where nature and technology meet. By pushing the boundaries of sustainable materials and composite craftsmanship, we strive to inspire limitless creativity—both in our clients and within ourselves.”
